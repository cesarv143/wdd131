Site Name

Azcapotzalco Boxing Club: This name represents the club's deep roots in the local community and its mission to empower individuals through the art of boxing and mental discipline.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a digital hub for the club, providing a platform for member registration, training schedules, attendance tracking, and motivational resources for athletes of all levels.

Target Audience Scenarios

"How can I register my child for the youth boxing program?"

"Where can I find the weekly training schedule and my attendance history?"
