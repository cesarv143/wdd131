Azcapotzalco Boxing Club

This name reflects the club’s deep roots in the Azcapotzalco community and its mission to empower individuals through boxing and discipline.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a digital hub for the Azcapotzalco Boxing Club, offering client registration, training schedules, attendance tracking, and motivational resources for members overcoming personal challenges.

Scenarios

  • How can I register my child for boxing classes?
  • Where can I find the weekly training schedule and attendance history?

Color Schema

  • Dark Red (#8B0000): Used for headers and accents to reflect strength and intensity.
  • Light Gray (#F5F5F5): Used for background and body text to maintain readability and contrast.

Typography

  • Oswald: Used for headings to convey boldness and clarity.
  • Roboto: Used for body text for clean readability across devices.
