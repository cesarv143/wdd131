Our Training Philosophy

At Azcapotzalco Boxing Club, training is about more than hitting a bag. We focus on proper footwork, defensive mastery, and cardiovascular endurance. Our facility is equipped with professional-grade rings and heavy bags to ensure you train like a champion.

What to Bring

  • Beginners: Comfortable athletic wear, water bottle, and clean indoor sneakers.
  • Intermediate/Advanced: 14oz or 16oz sparring gloves, mouthguard, and hand wraps are mandatory.
  • All Members: A positive attitude and readiness to work hard.
